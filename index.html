OverView

I came to know about Gaur Gopal Das Ji when I came across one of his videos on YouTube. I was really fascinated by his thoughts and her teachings. So, I researched books written by him and bought Life’s Amazing Secrets online from Amazon. I am really glad I did it because the insights, examples and the story are simple to understand and are life-changing if one truly understands the motive behind the same. So, in this book, Gaur Gopal Das Ji talks about the 4 principles that form the foundation of a happy life. These are Personal life, Relationships, Work life, and Social Contribution. He talks how to keep them balanced to live a life full of values and happiness.

Review

Overall, it’s been a book that I can pick up anytime I feel low or out of balance. It’s written beautifully and presented well. The language is quite easy and it is neither fast-paced nor slow. I read this book in 2 sittings as I was truly gripped by it and it wasn’t easy to put this down. So, I must say that it is quite engaging. I highly suggest you buy a Life’s Amazing Secrets. You won’t be disappointed at all.
